China's ex-foreign minister, who went missing, was removed over alleged affair According to a report in Wall Street Journal on Tuesday, China's former Foreign Minister Qin Gang, who was ousted from his position in July, had an extramarital affair while he was ambassador to the US.China's former Foreign Minister Qin Gang, who was ousted from his position in July, had an extramarital affair while he was ambassador to the United States, the Wall Street Journal reported on Tuesday, citing people familiar with a briefing. The report said Qin was cooperating with the investigation, which was now focused on whether the affair or Qin's conduct had compromised China's national security.Senior Chinese officials were told that an internal Communist Party investigation found Qin engaged in the affair throughout his tenure as China's ambassador to the United States, the report said. Two sources told the newspaper the affair had resulted in the birth of a child in the US.

Ronaldinho may visit Kolkata before Durga Puja

Ronaldinho, the Brazilian World Cup winner and two-time Ballon d’Or winner, is set to visit Kolkata in the second week of October. Sports promoter and business consultant Satadru Dutta confirmed the development to The Indian Express in an exclusive chat on Tuesday. Dutta, 43, has brought the likes of Maradona, Pele, Cafu and many more to Kolkata. He was also the brains behind reigning FIFA World Cup winner Emiliano Martinez’s visit to the City of Joy in July this year. “Multiple reports are circulating in the media about who will visit Kolkata, but I can tell the fans that I met Ronaldinho’s brother cum manager Assis and the plan is to bring the Brazilian great before the Durga Puja,” said Dutta. // Roberto de Assis Moreira, known as Assis, is a former footballer himself and Ronaldinho’s brother, manager and agent.
